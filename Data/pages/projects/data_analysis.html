Data Analysis

Valorant Champions 2022
Valorant is a fps game which holds world championships every year. On the weekend which they had the grand finals, I scrapped the tournaments data and analysed it. I tried and found some interesting analysis and KPIs to look at the player and team stats to determine how well the teams work each other, see how momentum based (emotional) the teams are, etc.

Twitter Hashtags
I often think that how twitter hashtags make us have a real affect on society by coming together and fight for the cause. But how often is a hashtag just toxic or preaches negativity. So in this project I try to analyse hashtags in a very general sense to they affect us and teaches about the views of people.
